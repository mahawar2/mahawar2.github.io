About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at Manipal University Jaipur. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering in May 2026 with the dissertation titled "Towards Sustainable Deep Learning Methods for Chest X-ray Diagnosis" .


My research interests include:

  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Explainable AI
  • Sustainable Artificial Intelligence

I am actively interested in collaborating with students, researchers, and industry professionals working in medical imaging and artificial intelligence.
